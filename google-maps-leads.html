Lead Generation

🗺️ Google Maps Lead Discovery

Searches Google Maps for local businesses in any niche, scrapes their websites for contact info, runs the data through Claude to extract company insights, and builds a ready-to-use lead list in Google Sheets.

The Problem

Finding local business leads manually — searching Google, clicking through websites, copying emails into a spreadsheet — takes hours and is completely soul-crushing. Most people give up after 20 leads.

The Solution

Apify's Google Places actor searches any business category in any city, returning hundreds of businesses with websites. A code node then scrapes those websites for emails and contact info. Claude extracts the relevant signals. Everything lands in Google Sheets, cleaned and ready for outreach.

Step by Step
🗺️

Apify Google Places Search

Searches for businesses by category and location — e.g. 'digital agency in Austin'. Returns names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and ratings.

🔎

Filter and Deduplicate

Removes businesses without websites, filters out excluded companies, deduplicates by domain.

🌐

Scrape Company Websites

For each business, scrapes the homepage and contact page looking for email addresses, team names, and company descriptions.

🧠

Claude Extracts Insights

Sends the scraped content to Claude to identify the business type, likely pain points, and contact person where possible.

📊

Save to Google Sheets

Full enriched record saved — business name, contact email, website, description, signal, and lead score.
