📈 Investor LP Report Automation

Pulls portfolio metrics from spreadsheets each month, formats them into a clean branded report, and sends personalized LP update emails automatically. No more copy-pasting numbers or formatting reports late at night.

The Problem

Monthly LP reports take 10-20 hours of manual work: pulling numbers from different spreadsheets, formatting them into a readable report, personalizing each update based on LP allocation, and sending them one by one. It's tedious and error-prone.

The Solution

On the first of each month, the workflow reads portfolio data from Google Sheets, pulls each LP's allocation and performance numbers, uses Claude to write a clear narrative summary of the month's performance, generates a formatted report, and sends each LP a personalized email with their specific numbers and context.

Step by Step
📅

Triggers on the 1st of Each Month

Runs automatically — no one needs to remember to do it or kick it off.

📊

Pull Portfolio Data from Sheets

Reads current portfolio performance, company updates, valuations, and any notable events from the master spreadsheet.

👤

Pull Each LP's Allocation

Reads each LP's ownership percentages and relevant holdings from a separate tab.

🧠

Claude Writes the Narrative

Given the raw numbers and events, Claude writes a concise, clear narrative for the month — what happened, what it means, what's next.

📄

Assemble Personalized Report

Each LP gets a report with their specific portfolio allocation, performance figures, and the narrative summary.

✉️

Send via Gmail

Personalized emails go out to each LP automatically. Subject lines and greetings are personalized.

📋

Log in Sheets

A record of each send is logged — who received it, when, and which version of the report.
