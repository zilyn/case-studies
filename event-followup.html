🎪 Event Follow-Up Engine

After any event, attendee data flows in automatically. Within 24 hours, every attendee gets a personalized follow-up based on which sessions they attended — no one falls through the cracks.

The Problem

Events generate a ton of warm leads, but most follow-up is either too slow, too generic, or never happens at all. Manually emailing 300 attendees with personalized messages is impossible. So teams either send a generic blast or do nothing.

The Solution

Attendee data from the event platform flows into the workflow automatically. Each attendee is segmented by which sessions they attended and any notes taken. Claude writes a personalized follow-up for each person that references their specific experience. Emails go out within 24 hours of the event ending, while the memory is still fresh.

Step by Step
📥

Attendee Data Flows In

Exported from the event platform or captured via webhook. Includes name, email, sessions attended, and any notes.

🏷️

Segment by Interest

Attendees are grouped by which sessions they attended or which topics they engaged with.

🧠

Claude Personalizes Each Email

Given the attendee's sessions and any notes, Claude writes a follow-up that feels human — referencing what they actually experienced at the event.

📧

Emails Sent Within 24 Hours

The whole batch goes out while the event is still top of mind. 30-second gap between sends to stay safe from spam filters.

📊

Log to CRM or Sheets

Every contact is logged with their engagement level, sessions attended, and follow-up sent — ready for the sales team.

🔔

Hot Leads Flagged

Attendees who engaged most (sessions attended, questions asked) are flagged for personal follow-up by the team.
