🧠 Enterprise RAG Knowledge Assistant

A secure internal AI assistant that lets employees ask plain-English questions about company documents, policies, contracts, and SOPs — and get accurate, cited answers in seconds.

The Problem

Companies have hundreds of internal documents but employees still email HR or spend 20 minutes searching for answers that are sitting in a PDF somewhere. Knowledge is siloed, and onboarding new staff is slow because they can't find anything.

The Solution

Documents are ingested, chunked, and stored in Weaviate as vector embeddings. When an employee asks a question, the system retrieves the most relevant chunks, feeds them to Claude with the query, and returns a clear answer with citations pointing back to the source document. Everything runs on internal infrastructure — no data leaves the company.

Step by Step
📄

Document Ingestion Pipeline

PDFs, Word docs, and text files are uploaded and automatically processed — chunked into passages, embedded, and stored in Weaviate.

🔄

Keeps Itself Updated

When documents are updated or new ones added, the pipeline re-indexes automatically. No manual maintenance.

💬

Employee Asks a Question

Via a simple chat interface or Slack bot. Plain English — no special syntax needed.

🔍

Vector Search in Weaviate

The question is embedded and matched against stored passages using semantic similarity. The top relevant chunks are retrieved.

🧠

Claude Generates the Answer

Claude receives the question and the retrieved passages, then writes a clear, concise answer — grounded only in the company's actual documents.

📎

Answer Includes Citations

Every answer includes which document and section the information came from, so employees can verify or dig deeper.
