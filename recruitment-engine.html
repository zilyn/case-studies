HR Automation

👥 Recruitment Data Enrichment Engine

Scrapes candidate profiles from LinkedIn and job boards, enriches them with public data, uses AI to classify skills and fit score, then pushes qualified candidates into the ATS automatically.

The Problem

Recruiters spend 60-70% of their time on manual work: searching for candidates, copying profile data into spreadsheets, and doing initial screening. Most of this doesn't require human judgment — it's repetitive data work.

The Solution

Apify scrapes candidate profiles based on role and skill criteria. The pipeline enriches each profile with additional public data, runs it through an AI model to score skill match and experience level against the job requirements, and pushes only the qualified candidates into Airtable as structured records ready for recruiter review.

Step by Step
🔍

Define Search Criteria

Role title, required skills, location, experience level — set once per job opening.

🕷️

Apify Scrapes Profiles

Searches LinkedIn, GitHub, and job boards for matching candidates. Returns structured profile data.

📊

Enrich with Public Data

Adds GitHub activity, portfolio links, publication history, and any other publicly available signals.

🤖

AI Scores Each Candidate

OpenAI evaluates each profile against the job requirements and assigns a fit score with reasoning. Filters out the bottom 60%.

📋

Push to Airtable ATS

Only candidates above the threshold are pushed — with their fit score, key strengths, and suggested interview questions.

📧

Notify Recruiter

A Slack message or email goes to the recruiter with a summary: X candidates found, Y qualified, top 3 highlighted.
